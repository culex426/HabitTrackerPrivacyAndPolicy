Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Habit Tracker collects and uses your information.

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

We do not store your data. Habit Tracker is an offline-first application. All data you enter (habits, goals, logs) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not have servers and cannot access your personal habit data.

2. Third-Party Services (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements, which helps keep this app free. AdMob may collect certain data to serve personalized ads, such as:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • App usage data

This data is managed entirely by Google. You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

View AdMob's Privacy Policy: Google AdMob

3. Permissions

We only request permissions necessary for core functionality:

  • Notifications & Alarms: Strictly for sending the habit reminders you schedule.
  • Internet: Strictly for loading ads. Your habit data is never transmitted.
